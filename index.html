Department of Mechatronics Engineering

‘Mechatronics’ is a synergic integration of Computer science, Mechanical, Electrical, & control engineering.

About Our Department

Vision of Department

Our vision is to produce quality mechatronics engineering professionals to face the challenges of emerging technologies of the world.

Mission of Department

Our mission is to:

  • To prepare learners to become technocrats, innovators and entrepreneurs.
  • To train learners to be employable in the field of mechatronics engineering.To contribute to the mechatronics engineering research.

Our Laboratories

Industry 4.0 lab

Robotics Lab

Equipment for analyzing and designing control systems, including PID controllers, MATLAB/Simulink workstations, and real-time control implementations.

Fabrcation Lab

Integrated workspace for full mechatronic system design, prototyping, and testing with 3D printers, laser cutters, and electronics workstations.
